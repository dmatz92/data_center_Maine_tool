Approvals it would need to clear

These are the main permitting hurdles, in plain terms. Most are triggered automatically by a facility this size; a few depend on the specific site.

How to read this. Burdens and benefits are shown side by side at the size you choose. The strength bars show how steeply each item grows with size—the core insight is that lasting benefits (permanent jobs) grow slowly while burdens (power, water) grow in step with size. No single "score" is shown on purpose.

Sources & limits. Power and water from facility-efficiency ranges (WUE/PUE, Lawrence Berkeley); Maine electricity use ~11,300 GWh/yr and sales tax 5.5% (EIA, Maine Revenue Services); equipment exemption per Maine's BETE program; build cost from 2025–26 industry benchmarks. Job and revenue multipliers are placeholders pending an IMPLAN or REMI model calibrated to Maine. Independent research finds data-center incentives have limited measurable effect on local jobs.

Not legal, engineering, or financial advice. For council discussion; verify against primary Maine statutes and a site-specific study before any decision.
